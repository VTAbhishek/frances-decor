Architectural Destination Floral Styling

"We believe in the understated power of romantic luxury—where each petal, light ray, and candle placement is choreographed to frame your personal legacy."

Frances Decor is Colombo's premier boutique floral design atelier. Specializing in architectural scale transformations and ultra-high-end destination weddings, we blend classical European romance with clean modern minimalism. Our creations are not mere decorations; they are immersive botanical landscapes built to capture deep emotional narratives.

Our Capabilities

Bespoke Styling Offerings

01

Floral Wedding Decor

Grand scale, architectural wedding stage installations, aisle pathways lined with floating botanicals, and customized floral wall setups designed around curated color profiles.

02

Destination Styling

Seamless design blueprint planning and structural execution across major resorts worldwide. We handle complex cargo operations, vendor logistics, and botanical sourcing.

03

Venue Transformation

Metamorphosing plain spaces, rustic gardens, and modern ballrooms into custom romantic garden cathedrals using fine sheer drapery, high-end production rigging, and candles.

04

Luxury Reception Design

Opulent mirror-topped banquet tablescapes, towering custom glass candelabras, handwritten calligraphy seatings, personalized napkins, and ambient dimmer lighting.

05

Bridal Bouquets

Bespoke hand-tied floral arrangements utilizing premium local orchids and delicate imported roses bound in cascading raw organic silk ribbons matching your wedding gown.

06

Stage & Aisle Design

Immersive wedding runways featuring high-gloss reflective walkways, delicate rows of rising calla lilies, and warm glowing tealight groupings extending to the horizon.
